Paraguay

Paraguay is a country in the heart of South America. It is bordered by Bolivia to the north, Argentina to the west and south, and Brazil to the east. The country is rich in nature with a nice tropical climate, has excellent gastronomy, and mixes Latin and indigenous cultures, which makes people open and friendly.
